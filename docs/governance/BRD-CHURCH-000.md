# BRD-CHURCH-000: Business Requirements Document - FaithHub

## 1.1 Purpose
Define the business problem, strategic intent, and success criteria for **FaithHub**, a multi-tenant SaaS platform that enables churches to deploy event hubs, invitations, PWAs, and domains with minimal technical friction.

## 1.2 Problem Statement
Churches currently rely on fragmented tools (Posters, Facebook, Google Forms, WhatsApp), leading to:
- Fragmented communications
- Poor RSVP tracking
- No canonical event page
- No installable app
- Manual follow-ups & reprinting costs

## 1.3 Business Objectives
- **Primary:** Enable churches to launch event hubs in <15 minutes, provide RSVP tools, and offer an installable PWA.
- **Strategic:** Vertical SaaS for Faith Organizations, starting with SDA.
- **Objectives:**
    - Provide RSVP + attendee exports
    - Support custom domains & PWA
    - Create recurring SaaS revenue

## 1.4 Target Customers
- **Primary:** Local churches, Clerks/Secretaries, Communications teams.
- **Secondary:** Conferences/Districts, Youth federations.
