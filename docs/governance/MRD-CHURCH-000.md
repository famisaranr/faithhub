# MRD-CHURCH-000: Market Requirements Document - FaithHub

## 2.1 Market Definition
- **Segment:** Protestant churches worldwide.
- **Beachhead:** SDA congregations (~90k).
- **Total Reachable:** 100k+ Protestant churches initially.

## 2.2 Use Cases
- Revival series
- Church anniversaries / centennials
- Youth week / Pathfinder events
- Health expos
- District fellowship events

## 2.3 Pricing Hypothesis
- **Starter ($10-15/mo):** Hosted URL, basic RSVP, 1 event.
- **Pro ($25-39/mo):** Custom Domain, PWA, Teams, Analytics, Unlimited events.
- **Conference ($150+/mo):** Enterprise dashboard.

## 2.4 Competitive Landscape
- **Indirect:** Wix, Google Forms, Facebook Events.
- **Direct:** Planning Center, Tithe.ly.
- **FaithHub Advantage:** Domain+PWA included, SDA specific templates, Budget-pricing.
