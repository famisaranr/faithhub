# FIRD-CHURCH-000: Financial & Investment Requirements Document

## 3.1 Investment Doctrine
- **Bootstrap-first:** Self-funded initially.
- **Cloud-only:** No on-premise headaches.
- **High Margin:** Usage-based infra, targeting >80% gross margin.

## 3.2 Unit Economics
- **Target ARPU:** $25/mo
- **COGS:** ~$3/mo
- **Gross Margin:** ~88%

## 3.3 Revenue Streams
1.  **Subscription:** Recurring monthly/annual fees.
2.  **Upsells:** Custom domains, SMS credits.
3.  **Services:** "Done-for-you" setup or white-labeling.

## 3.4 3-Year Targets
- **Year 1:** 150 churches → $45k ARR
- **Year 2:** 1,000 churches → $300k ARR
- **Year 3:** 3,500 churches → $1M+ ARR
